# حكايات خالد — شارة المقدمة (Suno AI script)

Intro theme for the series. Target length **~40–50s** (TV intro). Written for **Suno Custom Mode**
(model v4.5+). Default style is **nasheed: vocals + percussion only** (music policy pending the
sheikh's guidance — see compliance note); an instrumental-arrangement variant is included.

---

## 1) Title
```
حكايات خالد — شارة المقدمة
```

## 2) Style of Music — DEFAULT (compliance-safe nasheed)
```
Joyful Arabic children's nasheed, TV intro theme. VOCALS AND PERCUSSION ONLY — no melodic
instruments. Warm friendly male lead vocal with a bright happy children's choir in call-and-response,
hand claps, duff frame-drum groove, layered vocal harmonies carrying the melody, playful "la la la"
backing vocals, upbeat ~120 BPM, wholesome and heartwarming, very catchy singalong hook for ages
4-10, short 45-second TV opening, big final shout, clean tight ending.
```

Joyful Arabic children's TV intro theme, upbeat ~120 BPM. Warm friendly male lead + happy children's

## 7) How to generate (Suno)
1. **Custom Mode** ON → model **v4.5** (or newest). Paste Title / Style / Exclude / Lyrics.
2. Generate **3–4 takes**; pick the catchiest chorus. Kids must be able to hum it after one listen.
3. If a take runs long, use **Crop** (or just tell me — I trim/fade with ffmpeg).
4. **Save the winning take as a Persona** ("Hikayat Khalid Theme") → same voice for the outro,
   episode nasheeds, and jingles across the whole series.
5. Download **WAV**, drop it in `assets/music/` — I'll normalize to **-14 LUFS** and wire it into
   the intro template (brand sting + title card).
