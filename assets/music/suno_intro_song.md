## 7) Pronunciation & tashkeel notes (why it's written this way)
- **Line-end sukūn (waqf)** — words at line ends are written as SUNG (e.g. الْحِكَايَاتْ، مَكَانْ،
  نُورُهْ) so Suno doesn't invent case endings at pauses. Mid-line words keep full i'raab.
- **ة at pause** sounds like هْ (أَمَانَةْ ≈ "amaanah", الْقِطَّةْ ≈ "al-qittah") — correct sung Arabic.
- Names: خَالِدْ (KHAA-lid) · زِينَة (ZEE-na) · عَبْدُ الرَّحْمَنْ ('Abdur-Rah-MAAN) · تِيتَا (TEE-ta) ·
  جِدُّو (JID-do, doubled D).
- The (English parentheticals) are **performance cues** — Suno reads them as direction, not lyrics.

## 9) How to generate (Suno)
1. **Custom Mode** ON → model **v4.5+**. Paste Title / Style / Exclude / Lyrics exactly.
2. Generate **3–4 takes**; pick the catchiest chorus (kids should hum it after one listen).
3. Arabic garbled? Use the **transliteration fallback (§6)** — same tags and cues.
4. Long take → **Crop**, or hand it to me and I trim/fade with ffmpeg.
5. **Save the winner as a Persona** ("Hikayat Khalid Theme") → same voice for outros/jingles series-wide.
6. Download **WAV** → `assets/music/` → I normalize to **-14 LUFS** and wire it into the intro template.
